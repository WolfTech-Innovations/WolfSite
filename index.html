What's included

i3 + Latte Dock

Tiling window manager with macOS-style dock. Keyboard shortcuts and mouse-friendly.

UPX compressed

All binaries compressed with UPX --best --lzma. 50-70% size reduction, under 500MB ISO.

Steam ready

Pre-installed with Vulkan drivers and Proton for Windows games.

Touch support

Squeekboard on-screen keyboard. Works on handhelds, tablets, and 2-in-1s.

Apps included

Firefox, Discord, MangoHud, Dolphin, Alacritty, GParted.

Debian base

Bookworm stable. Rock-solid reliability, extensive repos.
